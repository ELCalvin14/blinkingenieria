What is SEON-IA?

An AI-powered educational app for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) — low internet required.

SEON-IA is propose to optimized AI for mobile devices to deliver offline educational activities in language, communication, and math, powered by Qualcomm Hexagon DSP and 8-bit models.

The Challenge

Across Latin America, over 5 million children are diagnosed with ASD, and many lack access to specialized educational tools.

SEON-IA removes barriers like internet access, cost, and distance — bringing inclusive technologies to every home.
  • Limited Access: Many areas lack tailored digital tools.
  • Connectivity Issues: SEON-IA works without an internet connection.
  • Financial Burden: No subscriptions or mobile data required.
